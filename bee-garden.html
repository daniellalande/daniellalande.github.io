Bee Garden

This project is part of an anthropological approach. Indeed, during three months we were interested in the impact that the meteorological changes had on the beekeeper's job. With this problem we conducted several interviews with beekeepers of different experiences in order to identify the problems they encounter in their beekeeping practices because of the weather.

The major problem was the lack of food available in nature for bees, due to the heat and the climate change that disrupts the seasons. Following this observation, we decided to create an application allowing individuals to put their garden at the disposal of beekeepers so that they can install their hives and thus feed their bees while allowing their garden to be maintained by the pollination of bees.
