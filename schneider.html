Schneider Electric

In the context of my last year of DN MADe digital, I had the chance to do an apprenticeship year in the Design Lab of Schneider Electric in Grenoble.

During this year, I was able to work on different projects as a UI/UX designer and motion designer. These projects - internal to the company - were mainly aimed at facilitating the use of certain services for technicians. The difficulties of these projects were on one hand, to ensure an ergonomic and graphic coherence between the numerous products that Schneider Electric offers, and on the other hand to work according to the different constraints that exist because of the link between application and physical product.
