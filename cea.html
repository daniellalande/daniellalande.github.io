CEA - Immersive device

As part of my fourth year of study in design, I had the opportunity to realize a project in collaboration with the CEA in Grenoble.

The request of this project was to realize an immersive device allowing to represent in a graphic way the role played by the CEA in the field of the cybersecurity. To do so, we decided to use the immersive projection room of the Yspot to project a large particle system representing, in an abstract way, a computer network in which malwares would hide.

The goal of the installation is to move in the digital system with the help of a tablet in order to find the different types of malwares hidden in the network.
