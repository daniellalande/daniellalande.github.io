Font Library

We were asked to redesign the "Font Library" website.
This typographic website is a community website based on open source principles.

Our redesign had to keep these aspects while offering a richer navigation and more current graphic codes. I decided to propose a huge canvas in order to move around the site and explore the available fonts.

The possibility of a search based on phrases rather than keywords would also bring a more human and natural side to the search.
